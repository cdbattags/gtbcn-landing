Frequently asked Questions

What is a hackathon?

Hackathons are where thoughts become things, collaborating, coding and creating literally anything. Attendees work in teams of up to four people for twenty-four hours to create a hack, web, app, hardware or anything.

When is HackGT@FIB?

HackGT@FIB will start the morning of Saturday, June 30 and continue until the July 1st.

What if I have more questions?

Tweet us at @hackupc or drop us a line at contact@hackupc.com

Do you have a code of conduct?

Yes. tl;dr: Be excellent to each other.

What if I don't have a team?

You need not to worry! Part of the fun of a hackathon is meeting new people. We will have time at the beginning of the event for everyone to meet and form teams. Everyone from first time to experienced hackers will find their perfect match.

What can I build?

Web, mobile, hardware, anything is on the table. Projects will be judged based on creativity, technical difficulty, polish, and usefulness by a panel of industry judges.

Where is it being held?

HackGT@FIB will take place in sunny Barcelona, Spain.

Who can attend?

Students from universities and high schools from all around the world.

How much does it cost?

HackGT@FIB is free for all accepted hackers. We will provide your WiFi, meals, swag, and workspace.

What should I bring?

All attendees must bring a valid ID for admission. Other common checklist items include: laptops, sleeping bags, chargers and toiletries. We strongly recommend comfy shoes and pants for the night :3

How big can teams be?

Teams of up to four are welcome. You can also hack solo if you want!
